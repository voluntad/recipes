Ingredients:
- 6 sheets nori
- 3 c. sushi rice
- 6 c. water
- 12 pieces tempura shrimp
- 1 small avocado
- 1 bunch green onion
- 6 slices bacon

Directions:

1. In large pot combine sushi rice and water; bring to a boil, cover, and reduce h
   eat. Simmer 15-20 minutes or until rice is fluffy and tender. Remove from heat 
   and let sit 10 minutes, set aside to cool.

2. Bake tempura shrimp as directed on the package.. Cut off tails and slice shrimp
   in half lengthwise; set aside.

3. Peel and pit avocado. Slice into thin slices and set aside.

4. Prepare green onions by cutting off bottoms and slicing into thin lengths. Set 
   aside.

5. In large frying pan fry bacon until crispy; drain on paper towels and set aside.

6. Prepare sushi as follows: cover a bamboo sushi mat with plastic wrap and lay a 
   sheet of nori, rough side up, on wrap. Firmly pat a thick, even layer of rice
   over 
   nori. Place 1 long piece green onion, a layer of avocado, two pieces tempura 
   shrimp, and 1 piece of bacon on sushi roll.

7. Pick up edge of nori up , fold the bottom of the nori up , enclosing the filling 
   and tightly roll the sushi into a thick cylinder. Once the sushi is rolled, wrap 
   in the mat and gently squeeze to seal. Cut each roll into 6 pieces and 
   refrigerate 2 hours or until ready to serve. Repeat until all ingredients are 
   used.
